About the game

About Space Adventure: Noobiks Battle vs Zombies

Space Adventure: Noobiks Battle vs Zombies places a Minecraft-style noob inside a space station full of cosmic undead. Progress is tied to destroying crystals, unlocking doors, and surviving each new platform challenge while the station grows more dangerous.

Why it fits

Why this mission belongs in the library

It adds visual variety to the zombie cluster without leaving the action niche. The sci-fi setting and platform movement make the page feel different while keeping the same undead combat hook.
