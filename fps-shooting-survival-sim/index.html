About the game

About FPS Shooting Survival Sim

FPS Shooting Survival Sim throws you straight into a zombie outbreak where staying alive matters more than flashy movement. It leans into a classic desktop shooter rhythm: move, aim, reposition, and clear threats before the wave closes around you.

Survival loop

Why the survival loop works

This one feels heavier than a quick arcade shooter because positioning matters. Sprint at the wrong moment and you overcommit, hesitate and the horde stacks up, and one bad angle can turn a manageable fight into a full collapse.

Tips

How to stay alive longer

  • Keep moving instead of holding one lane for too long.
  • Use sprint bursts to rebuild distance when zombies close in.
  • Read the next opening before you commit to a turn or jump.
  • Do not waste shots when you can funnel enemies into cleaner lines.

Game controls

Controls

  • WASD: walk
  • Space: jump
  • Mouse: aim and shoot
  • Shift + W: run
