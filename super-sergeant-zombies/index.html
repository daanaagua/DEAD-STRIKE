About the game

An arcade soldier fantasy dropped into undead combat

Super Sergeant Zombies pushes the heroic military angle harder than some survival pages. The fun comes from feeling like a heavily armed lead fighter while still dealing with the fast mistakes and constant horde pressure that make zombie shooters satisfying.

Why it fits

It keeps the tone energetic instead of purely grim

This mission gives the zombie category a more arcade-forward option. That matters because some players want undead action without every page leaning all the way into horror or hopeless survival atmosphere.
