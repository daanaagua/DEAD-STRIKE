About the game

About Dead Strike

Dead Strike is a first-person zombie shooter survival game where players fight against hordes of zombies in a post-apocalyptic world. Go into battles across ruined cities, combat relentless undead waves, and complete increasingly challenging missions while fighting for survival and humanity's last hope.

Mission pressure

Face non-stop zombie attacks and survive

Taking on the role of a sniper sent to the warfare frontier, your job is to survive incoming attacks, eliminate every enemy in the zone, and keep the city from falling. Zombies can approach from any direction, so quick aiming, disciplined reloads, and panic-free movement all matter.

Progression

Progress through tougher missions

Travel across multiple city locations, defend key areas, and push into harder objectives. As missions ramp up, zombie waves get denser and tougher to stop. Upgrade weapons, improve ammo, and use bandages wisely if you want to survive longer runs.

Game controls

Rank up and improve your loadout

  • Mouse: aim weapon
  • Left click: fire
  • R: reload ammo
  • Q: switch weapons
  • G: throw a grenade
  • F: use a bandage
  • Tab: switch direction
