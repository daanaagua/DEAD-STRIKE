About the game

About Dead Strike

Play Dead Strike online as a fast-paced zombie FPS where every mission throws fresh undead pressure at you. Fight through infected zones, clear each wave, and keep advancing by unlocking stronger guns and staying sharp under fire.

Mission pressure

Face non-stop zombie attacks and survive

Dead Strike rewards quick reflexes and precise shooting. Zombies come in waves, some rushing straight at you while others pressure you from range, so every reload, sidestep, and headshot matters when the arena starts to close in.

Progression

Unlock stronger weapons and survive longer

Each run pushes you deeper into harder missions with thicker waves and less room for mistakes. Upgrade early, learn how each zombie behaves, and use better weapons to stay alive when the later fights turn chaotic.

Game controls

Master the controls before the next wave

  • W / A / S / D: move
  • Mouse movement: aim
  • Mouse click: shoot
  • Shift: sprint
  • Spacebar: jump or dodge
  • Keys 1-6: switch weapons
