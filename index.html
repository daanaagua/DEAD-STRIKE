About the game

About DEAD STRIKE

Dead Strike is a first-person zombie shooter survival game set in a ruined world where every mission throws you into direct combat against relentless undead waves. The job is simple on paper and brutal in practice: aim fast, shoot cleanly, finish the objective, and keep the city from collapsing completely.

Mission pressure

Face non-stop zombie attacks

You fight as a frontline shooter dropped into city sectors that are already overrun. Zombies can push in from multiple directions, and once they get close they start stripping away your health immediately. That makes quick aiming, constant scanning, and disciplined reload timing more important than raw aggression.

Progression

Progress through tougher missions

Missions move you through different parts of the city and steadily raise the pressure. Later runs throw tougher enemies, denser attack waves, and less room for sloppy weapon management. To last longer, you need stronger firepower, smarter ammo use, and timely healing when the undead close the gap.

Game controls

Rank up and improve your loadout

Finishing missions helps you rank up, unlock better weapons, and improve your chances against heavier zombie variants. Better gear matters here because harder enemies absorb more punishment and punish mistakes much faster.

  • Mouse: aim weapon
  • Left click: fire
  • R: reload ammo
  • Q: switch weapons
  • G: throw grenade
  • F: use bandage
  • Tab: switch direction
