About the game

About Sniper Master

Sniper Master gives the site a cleaner shooting rhythm. Instead of managing long zombie waves, the fun here comes from timing, angle control, and landing the right shot when the window opens.

Why it works

Precision over chaos

After heavier survival shooters, this page feels lighter and faster. The pressure is still real, but it is created by accuracy and timing rather than crowd control, ammo panic, or a collapsing lane full of undead.

Tips

How to score cleaner shots

  • Wait for a stable line instead of firing too early.
  • Use short sessions to learn the shot rhythm fast.
  • Treat movement and firing as one timing problem, not two separate actions.
  • Reset after misses because rushed follow-up shots usually collapse.

Game controls

Controls

  • Mouse click: fire
  • Tap: fire on mobile
