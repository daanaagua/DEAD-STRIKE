About the game

Voxel visuals with a true sniper rhythm

Counter Craft Sniper mixes a blocky look with a cleaner marksmanship loop. The style makes the page approachable, but the real draw is still aim discipline, target timing, and the satisfaction of solving combat at range instead of with panic sprays.

Why it fits

Good bridge between craft-style art and serious long shots

This mission expands the sniper branch without making it feel repetitive. It gives players a softer visual package while preserving the precision-first intent that makes sniper pages useful for the overall library.

Game controls

Controls

The game follows familiar desktop shooter controls for quick pickup.

  • WASD: move
  • Left click: shoot
  • Right click: scope
  • R: reload
  • Space: jump
