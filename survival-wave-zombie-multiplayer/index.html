About the game

About Survival Wave Zombie Multiplayer

Survival Wave Zombie Multiplayer mixes soldier firefights and realistic zombie pressure inside one browser shooter. The appeal is variety: gun game, survival, team deathmatch, deathmatch, and multiplayer sessions all push the action in slightly different directions.

Why it fits

Why this mission belongs in the library

This page fits the site because it extends the zombie FPS angle into a more mode-heavy experience. Instead of one survival loop, you get multiple ways to fight, test weapon handling, and jump between AI pressure and more competitive pacing.
