About the game

About Guns vs Magic

Guns vs Magic drops you into the role of Silvius, a student of magic trying to stop a former mentor who has fallen to a cursed crystal. The result is a shooter-adventure hybrid where firearms, spell energy, and fantasy enemies all share the same battlefield.

Why it fits

Why this mission belongs in the library

This game expands the site without leaving the action lane. It still gives players aiming, shooting, and progression, but wraps those mechanics in a fantasy setting that breaks up the military-zombie rhythm.
